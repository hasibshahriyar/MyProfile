Current Research

EchoShield: Detecting AI-Generated Voices in the Modern Soundscape

Active Research

2024 - Present

The paper presents EchoShield, an advanced framework for detecting AI-generated voices (deepfake audio) by combining CNNs, RNNs, and Transformers. It effectively addresses the limitations of traditional detection methods by leveraging hybrid models and sophisticated feature extraction techniques. The system demonstrated high accuracy and scalability across diverse datasets, making it a robust tool for audio forensics and security.

Key Aspects:

  • EchoShield Framework for deepfake audio detection.
  • Hybrid deep learning models (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers).
  • Diverse datasets and advanced preprocessing techniques.
  • Evaluation using metrics like F1-score, recall, and EER.
